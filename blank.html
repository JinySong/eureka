Frequently Asked Questions

Is your showroom open to the public? Is an appointment required?


Our showroom is open to the public. We have a collection of kitchen cabinets and islands, bathroom vanities, light fixtures, and shelving units for your viewing. There is no appointment necessary to visit and you may drop in any time during our business hours. We are located at 15-505 Iroquois Shore Rd, Oakville Ontario.

How long is your production time?


It takes around 6-8 weeks to produce cabinets.

Do you have a warranty?


We offer a 12- month warranty on the custom-made cabinets. The warranty covers the labor, as well as the installation of the entirety of your renovation project. A limited lifetime warranty on hardware will be provided by Eureka hardware suppliers as well.

Can I get a free quote?


Yes. Eureka offers a free initial consultation where our agents will come into your home and measure your space.

Does Eureka offer on-site measurements?


Eureka offers free on-site measurements.

Does Eureka offer 3D design or Rendering?


Yes. We provide both 3D design and rendering.

Does Eureka offer refacing and resurfacing?


Yes, we do refacing and resurfacing.

Does your company have proper insurance？ Are you licensed?


We are a fully insured company, which means that we carry over $2 million in liability coverage. Clients will never be held liable for any harm or accident that occurs on our job site. Our staffs are all highly trained, and we take serious internal measures to make sure that they are doing everything possible to avoid dangerous situations.

Will I need permits?


You will need a permit if there are major structural changes, such as additions or extensions of an electrical or plumbing variety. If your renovations are mostly decorative or aesthetic in nature or if they are adding or modifying internal functions, such as new cabinets or countertops, you will not require a permit. We’re here to help if you have never gone through the process of obtaining a permit.
