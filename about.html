About Eureka Kitchen & Bath

When you pick Eureka to remodel your kitchen or bathroom, you are choosing a team with more than 25 years of industry experience. Our crew of designers, constructors, project managers, and administration staff will make sure your project is completed to industry standards, on-time, and on-budget.

Because we order materials from the manufacturer directly, we pass on the savings to our clients. The manufacturing time is approximately 6 to 8 weeks, depending on the project.

Contact us to set up a free consultation and our consultants will walk you through all the steps, including designs, materials, prices, and project timeline. The initial consultation is free, and there's no pressure to buy from us. We want you to be informed so you may experience for yourself how we stand out from our competitors.

We are here for you to change your home to the one of your dreams.

Quality Work From Quality Experts:

Eureka Kitchen & Bath provides a full spectrum of renovation services, including bathrooms, kitchens, and basements, to ensure optimum utilization of space and deliver aesthetically pleasing results. Right from planning and design to project management and through all phases of construction, we will be present to make your project a successful one. From baths and kitchens to whole-house plans, we can transform any room to add more value to your place.

Our areas of expertise include custom home renovation, bathroom and kitchen remodelling, and all kinds of organization and interior design needs. We have the resources and knowledge to handle projects of any size and will deliver what we promise. Our transparent process, inclusive and honest approach and friendly and accessible team will ensure you get the desired results.
